Experience California

Cities and Places to Visit

San Francisco

  • Located on the Northern California coast, and is the financial and tech capital of the state.
  • Things to do and see:
    • The Golden Gate Bridge
    • Haight Ashbury
    • Dolores Park
    • Fisherman's Wharf
    • Alcatraz
    • Ocean Beach and the Land's End Trail
    • Muir Woods

Los Angeles

  • Located in Southern California. Is California' largest city and its entertainment capital.
  • Things to do and see:
    • Hollywood
    • Disney Land
    • Six Flags Magic Mountain
    • Santa Monica

Sacramento

  • Located in Northern California and is the capital of California.
  • Things to do and see:
    • The State Capitol
    • The Sacramento River
    • Old Sacramento
    • The American River

Redwoods

  • California's redwood forests are a state treasure. These amazing trees can grow to 100 meters in height and can live for thousand's of years.
  • Things to do and see:
    • Redwood National and State Parks
    • The Avenue of the Giants

Sacramento

  • Lake Tahoe is the second deepest lake in the United States. It is also among the oldest and the most pristine. It is California's blue jewel as it has a deep blue color and offers world class skiing and snowboarding resorts in the winter.
  • Things to do and see:
    • Squaw Valley Ski Resort
    • South Lake Tahoe
    • Day cruises through the beautiful Lake Tahoe
